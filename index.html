Our Mission

HELP EVERYONE
REMAIN OPERATIONAL®

The HERO Foundation is a Nevada nonprofit organization established to honor and support veterans, first responders, nurses, teachers, EMTs, police officers, and firefighters through water-based healing, education, and vocational training. The Foundation promotes the restorative power of scuba diving, free diving, and aquatic environments to improve mental health, resilience, and operational readiness.

Through structured training, scholarships, and outreach opportunities, the HERO Foundation helps participants develop leadership skills, career pathways, and renewed purpose through the healing properties of water.

Core Programs

WHERE YOUR
SUPPORT GOES

Every program is built around the healing power of water — from therapeutic dives to certified training pathways, we meet heroes where they are and help them move forward.

🤿

Therapeutic Dive Sessions

Therapeutic scuba and freediving sessions designed to improve mental health, reduce stress, and restore resilience for veterans, first responders, and service personnel.

🌊

Peer-Support Dives

Structured peer-support and mentorship dives that build community, trust, and connection among those who have served — because healing is better together.

🎓

Dive Apprenticeship & Certification

Dive apprenticeship and certification programs that open vocational pathways, turning a therapeutic experience into a career opportunity or lifelong skill.

📚

Educational Workshops

Workshops focused on stress recovery, ocean awareness, and personal development — providing tools that extend well beyond the water.

🎖️

HERO Scholarship Program

Scholarships for veterans, first responders, nurses, teachers, EMTs, police officers, and firefighters pursuing dive certifications, education, and professional development.

🤝

Community Outreach

Outreach opportunities connecting heroes with leadership development, career pathways, and a network of peers who understand the mission because they lived it.

Our Impact

MISSION
RESULTS

01

Direct Financial Relief

Funds are directly distributed in emergency grants to veterans and first responders facing housing, medical, and family crises across the country.

02

Education Funded

Scholarships awarded to veterans and their families pursuing degrees, certifications, and trade programs to build new careers after service.

Businesses Launched

Veteran entrepreneurs guided through our entrepreneurship pipeline have launched sustainable businesses that now employ fellow veterans in their communities.

04

A growing network of heroes supporting heroes — peer mentors, business partners, and friends who understand the mission because they lived it.
